About GreenWood Furniture

We manufacture eco-friendly wooden furniture using premium quality woods. Our furniture is handcrafted with traditional designs and modern finishing.

Our Products

A curated range of handcrafted furniture built to last.

Bedroom

Solid wood cots, bedframes and headboards in teak, mango and mahogany.

Living Room

Comfortable sofas, coffee tables and TV units designed for daily life.

Dining

Dining tables, benches and chairs crafted for family gatherings.

Our Process

We source responsibly managed timber and use low-VOC finishes. Each piece is inspected by hand to ensure strength, fit and finish before it ships.

Sustainability

We prioritise reclaimed and FSC-certified timber where possible and offset waste through local reuse programs. Sustainability is central to our design and manufacturing choices.
